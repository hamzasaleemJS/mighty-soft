KEY FEATURES

Qualities that our product have.

Exquisite Quilting for Added Comfort

The 4Inch thick mattress topper adds an additional layer of
plushness and comfort to your bedding. Quilted design
ensures equal distribution of fiber filling for fuller loft so you
can enjoy a comfortable sleep all night long

Fits like a Glove

Mighty Soft soft mattress topper fits snugly around the
mattress with the help of four durable elastic straps on each
corner that does not rip or tear easily

Cost-Effective & Luxurious

An affordable solution to revitalize your existing mattress.
Mighty Soft 10cm mattress topper is thick and plump to give
you the luxurious hotel-like feel and does not produce any
creaking sound, to minimize sleep disruptions, ensuring a
good night’s sleep

Wash Confidently

High quality material and durable stitching keeps it as it is
even after several washes. Machine wash at 40°C using mild
detergent. Tumble dry on low heat to avoid fading and
stretching

Cozy & Breathable for Deeper Sleep

Made of microfiber for adequate long-lasting cushioning,
comfortable conforming and even support. The odor-free and
breathable material makes it one of the best mattress topper
which promotes airflow to maintain the optimal temperature,
offering you a relaxed sleep

Cozy & Breathable for Deeper Sleep (Double Mattress)

Made of Hollow fiber as inner filling and Micro fiber as outer
fabric for adequate long-lasting cushioning, comfortable
conforming and even support. The odor-free and breathable
material of Mighty Soft double mattress topper promotes
airflow to maintain the optimal temperature, offering you a
relaxed and peaceful sleep
